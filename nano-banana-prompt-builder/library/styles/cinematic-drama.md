# Cinematic Drama

## Plain English
Movie poster quality with dramatic lighting - makes anyone look like an epic hero. Think Marvel movies, action blockbusters, powerful moments frozen in time.

## When to Use
- Superhero transformations
- Powerful professional portraits
- Movie poster style images
- Epic fantasy scenes
- Anyone who should look heroic/powerful

## Key Ingredients

### Camera
- **Lens**: 85mm (flattering, cinematic)
- **Angle**: Low angle looking UP (makes subject powerful)
- **Framing**: Medium shot or full body with environment
- **Depth**: Shallow depth of field, subject sharp, background soft

### Lighting
- **Style**: High contrast, dramatic shadows
- **Direction**: Strong side lighting or rim light from behind
- **Quality**: Hard light for drama, soft fill for faces
- **Effects**: Lens flares welcome, volumetric light, god rays

### Textures & Details
- Fabric movement (capes flowing, hair in wind)
- Environmental particles (dust, sparks, rain)
- Reflective surfaces catching light
- Rich, saturated colors

## Pro Tips
1. Low angle is KEY - it's what makes subjects look powerful
2. Rim lighting separates subject from background dramatically
3. Environmental elements (wind, particles) add life
4. Don't be afraid of strong shadows - they create drama
5. Include something moving (cape, hair, particles) for energy
