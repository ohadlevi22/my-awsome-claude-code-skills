# Fine Art Conceptual

## Plain English
Gallery-worthy artistic imagery with symbolic meaning - like a museum piece or art exhibition photograph. Creates images that make people think and feel, with intentional symbolism and refined aesthetic.

## When to Use
- Artistic self-expression
- Symbolic/meaningful portraits
- Gallery or exhibition concepts
- Creative personal projects
- Images with deeper meaning

## Key Ingredients

### Camera
- **Lens**: 50mm (neutral, truthful) or medium format feel
- **Angle**: Neutral to slightly elevated, intentional framing
- **Framing**: Carefully composed with negative space
- **Depth**: Variable - sometimes sharp throughout, sometimes selective

### Lighting
- **Style**: Controlled, intentional, often minimal
- **Direction**: Dramatic single source or carefully balanced
- **Quality**: Clean, refined, gallery-appropriate
- **Effects**: Subtle reflections, controlled shadows as design elements

### Textures & Details
- Symbolic objects and props
- Intentional color palette (often limited)
- Clean, minimal backgrounds
- High production value in every element

## Example Prompts

### Glass Sphere Portrait
```
An artistic portrait where this person appears enclosed within a
transparent glass sphere or bubble,
maintaining their exact facial features with artistic clarity.

The sphere is elegant and minimal, with refined reflections
and subtle light refraction.

The person inside looks confident and serene, sharply focused.

The background is minimalist, studio-like, with soft gradients
and dramatic yet clean lighting.

The image feels modern, luxurious, and symbolic - like a cover image
or gallery artwork.

Fine art contemporary portrait quality.
Studio lighting with controlled reflections.
No fantasy creatures, no surreal distortion.
Ultra quality, museum exhibition worthy.
```

### Marionette Self-Portrait
```
A high-end conceptual fashion portrait of this woman acting as
a marionette puppeteer.
She is wearing a tailored red suit, a deep blue shirt underneath,
and a soft blue ribbon tied around her neck, elegant and confident.

In front of her, suspended by visible thin strings, is a marionette
puppet designed as a stylized version of herself.
The marionette is clearly a wood-and-fabric puppet, with articulated
joints, slightly exaggerated proportions, and visible craftsmanship -
wooden limbs, painted face, fabric clothing.

The puppet wears a miniature red suit with blue accents, echoing
her outfit, but in a clearly theatrical, handcrafted style.
She is holding the control cross above, actively manipulating the strings -
showing control, intention, and performance.

The relationship feels artistic, symbolic, and playful - more theatre,
identity, and self-reflection than realism.

Lighting is cinematic and refined, highlighting textures:
wood grain, fabric, stitching, and tailored clothing.
Background is a dark, painterly studio backdrop, elegant and atmospheric.

Mood: theatrical elegance, self-awareness, creative control.
Camera: medium to full portrait, eye-level or slightly low angle.
Style: editorial fashion photography meets fine-art theatre imagery.
Ultra-detailed, high resolution, professional studio quality.

Avoid: lifelike dolls, human replicas, porcelain realism, uncanny or horror aesthetics
```

### Floating Elements Portrait
```
Transform this photo into a surreal fine art portrait,
this person in a meditative pose with eyes closed,
maintaining their exact facial features with artistic softness.

Delicate objects float gently around them in suspended animation:
vintage keys, dried flowers, torn book pages, small origami birds.

Each floating element casts its own soft shadow,
creating depth and dimension in the composition.

Single dramatic side light creates painterly shadows on face,
highlighting the peaceful expression.

Deep charcoal gradient background, minimal and gallery-appropriate.
Limited color palette: warm skin tones against cool grays.

Fine art portrait photography, dreamlike and contemplative atmosphere.
Museum exhibition quality, intentional negative space,
symbolic elements suggesting memory and imagination.
8K resolution, every floating element sharply detailed.
```

## Negative Prompt Defaults
```
fantasy creatures, childish style, heavy busy textures, cluttered background,
over-saturated colors, cartoonish elements, obvious digital effects,
stock photo feeling, generic composition, unintentional elements,
horror aesthetic, disturbing imagery, creepy uncanny elements,
low artistic value, snapshot quality, casual composition
```

## Pro Tips
1. Every element should have intentional meaning or purpose
2. Negative space is a compositional tool, not emptiness
3. Limited color palettes create cohesion and impact
4. Symbolism should be accessible but not obvious
5. Technical perfection serves the artistic vision
6. The concept/meaning should be clear without explanation
