# Soft & Dreamy

## Plain English
Warm, gentle, romantic imagery with soft golden lighting - like the best family photos you've ever seen. Perfect for making loved ones look beautiful in tender, emotional scenes.

## When to Use
- Family portraits and group photos
- Children in magical/playful scenes
- Romantic couple imagery
- Nostalgic or sentimental moments
- Any scene that should feel warm and loving

## Key Ingredients

### Camera
- **Lens**: 50mm (natural, intimate) or 85mm (flattering portraits)
- **Angle**: Eye-level (relatable) or slightly above for children
- **Framing**: Close-up to medium shot, focus on faces and emotions
- **Depth**: Soft background blur, dreamy bokeh

### Lighting
- **Style**: Soft, diffused, warm tones
- **Direction**: Golden hour backlight or soft window light
- **Quality**: No harsh shadows, gentle gradients
- **Effects**: Lens flare from sun, light rays, golden particles

### Textures & Details
- Soft fabrics (flowing dresses, cozy sweaters)
- Natural elements (flowers, grass, leaves)
- Warm color palette (golds, soft pinks, creamy whites)
- Genuine expressions and natural poses

## Pro Tips
1. Golden hour lighting (or simulated) is the secret ingredient
2. Backlight creates beautiful rim lighting and hair glow
3. Keep backgrounds simple and soft - the subject is the star
4. Natural expressions beat posed smiles every time
5. Warm color palette unifies the image emotionally
6. Soft fabrics and natural textures enhance the dreamy feel
