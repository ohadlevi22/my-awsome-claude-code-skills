# Editorial Fashion

## Plain English
Magazine cover quality - clean, polished, and striking. Makes anyone look like they belong on the cover of Vogue or a professional business magazine. Perfect for professional headshots and glamour imagery.

## When to Use
- Professional headshots and corporate portraits
- Fashion-style personal branding
- Magazine cover concepts
- Glamour and beauty shots
- High-end product with people

## Key Ingredients

### Camera
- **Lens**: 85mm f/1.4 (flattering, beautiful bokeh)
- **Angle**: Eye-level or slightly below (confident, powerful)
- **Framing**: Head and shoulders, or 3/4 body
- **Depth**: Shallow DOF, creamy background separation

### Lighting
- **Style**: Clean, controlled, three-point studio setup
- **Direction**: Main light 45 degrees, fill light opposite, rim light from behind
- **Quality**: Soft but defined, sculpting the face
- **Effects**: Catchlights in eyes, subtle rim highlighting hair

### Textures & Details
- Flawless skin (but not plastic)
- Sharp detail on clothing texture
- Clean, simple backgrounds (gradients, solid colors)
- Refined makeup and styling

## Pro Tips
1. Catchlights in eyes are essential - they bring life to portraits
2. Rim light separates subject from background elegantly
3. Slightly below eye-level makes subject look more powerful
4. Clean backgrounds keep focus on the person
5. Sharp clothing texture contrasts with soft skin
6. Confidence in pose matters more than complexity
