# Lighting Guide

Plain English explanations of lighting for non-photographers.

## Lighting by Mood

### What Feeling Do You Want?

| Mood | Lighting Style | Key Characteristics | Best For |
|------|----------------|---------------------|----------|
| **Warm & Cozy** | Golden hour | Soft orange glow, long shadows | Family, romance, nostalgia |
| **Dramatic & Powerful** | High contrast | Strong shadows, bright highlights | Heroes, action, intensity |
| **Professional & Clean** | Studio three-point | Even, flattering, controlled | Headshots, corporate, fashion |
| **Mysterious & Moody** | Low-key | Mostly dark, selective highlights | Drama, noir, artistic |
| **Bright & Happy** | High-key | Minimal shadows, bright and airy | Joy, innocence, commercial |
| **Magical & Dreamy** | Soft backlight | Glowing edges, ethereal feel | Fantasy, whimsy, wonder |
| **Cool & Modern** | Blue hour / tech glow | Cool tones, screen light | Technology, night, urban |

---

## Natural Light Types

### Time of Day Lighting

| Time | Name | Quality | Mood |
|------|------|---------|------|
| **Sunrise/Sunset** | Golden Hour | Warm orange, soft, directional | Romantic, nostalgic, beautiful |
| **Just after sunset** | Blue Hour | Cool blue, soft, atmospheric | Moody, peaceful, cinematic |
| **Midday** | Harsh Sun | Hard shadows, high contrast | Dramatic but challenging |
| **Overcast** | Soft Daylight | Even, diffused, no harsh shadows | Natural, flattering, soft |
| **Shade** | Open Shade | Soft, even, slightly cool | Portraits, soft beauty |

**Prompt language:**
- "golden hour sunlight creating warm rim lighting"
- "soft overcast lighting with no harsh shadows"
- "blue hour twilight with cool atmospheric tones"

## Studio Lighting Setups

### Common Patterns

| Setup | Description | Effect | Best For |
|-------|-------------|--------|----------|
| **Three-point** | Key + Fill + Rim | Balanced, professional | Most portraits |
| **Rembrandt** | Triangle of light on cheek | Classic, artistic | Traditional portraits |
| **Butterfly** | Light from directly above | Glamorous, sculpted | Beauty, fashion |
| **Split** | Light from side, half face dark | Dramatic, mysterious | Mood, character |
| **Rim/Backlight** | Light from behind subject | Glowing edge, separation | Separation, drama |

**Prompt language:**
- "three-point studio lighting, soft key light at 45 degrees"
- "Rembrandt lighting with classic triangle on cheek"
- "strong rim light creating glowing edge separation"

---

## Light Quality

### Hard vs Soft Light

| Type | Look | Created By | Best For |
|------|------|------------|----------|
| **Hard light** | Sharp defined shadows, high contrast | Direct sun, small lights | Drama, texture, definition |
| **Soft light** | Gentle gradual shadows, low contrast | Clouds, large diffusers | Flattering, gentle, beauty |

**Prompt language:**
- "soft diffused lighting wrapping around the face"
- "hard directional light creating defined shadows"

---

## Special Lighting Effects

### Cinematic & Creative

| Effect | Description | When to Use |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| **Lens flare** | Light streaks from bright source | Epic moments, energy |
| **Volumetric/God rays** | Visible light beams through atmosphere | Magical, dramatic, atmospheric |
| **Practical lights** | Real lights visible in scene | Authenticity, night scenes |
| **Neon glow** | Colored light from signs/screens | Urban, cyberpunk, modern |
| **Candlelight** | Warm, flickering, intimate | Romance, period, intimate |
| **Firelight** | Warm, dancing shadows | Adventure, camping, drama |

**Prompt language:**
- "volumetric light rays filtering through trees"
- "subtle lens flare from the setting sun"
- "neon signs casting colorful reflections"

## Lighting Direction

### Where Light Comes From

| Direction | Effect | Mood |
|-----------|--------|------|
| **Front** | Flat, even, few shadows | Safe, clear, documentary |
| **45° Side** | Sculpted, dimensional | Classic, flattering |
| **Side (90°)** | Split, dramatic | Mysterious, intense |
| **Back** | Silhouette, rim glow | Dramatic, mysterious, heroic |
| **Above** | Butterfly shadows under nose/chin | Glamorous, sculpted |
| **Below** | Spooky, unnatural | Horror, drama (use rarely) |

## Quick Reference by Style

| Style | Primary Lighting | Secondary | Effects |
|-------|-----------------|-----------|---------|
| Cinematic Drama | Hard side/rim light | Deep shadows | Lens flares, volumetric |

| Fine Art Conceptual | Controlled single source | Intentional shadows | Minimal, refined |
